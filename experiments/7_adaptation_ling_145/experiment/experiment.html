InterActive Language Processing Lab at Stanford

In this experiment, you will see a series of visual scenes and will be asked to answer some questions about some of them. You will need to listen what the people in the video have to say, so please make sure that your sound is turned on. We recommend Google Chrome or Firefox. Please pay attention, thanks!

Instructions

On the following pages, you will see a scene with an adult and a girl standing next to a table with a gumball machine on it. Importantly, the girl cannot see the gumball machine as it is too high up for her to see. The adult can see all the gumballs in the machine, which are constantly being tossed around.

After observing each scene, consider the two sentences below the scene and rate how likely you think it is that the adult would say each of the two sentences. If you think that both answers are unlikely, you can indicate this by giving the third option, something else, a high rating. To rate the sentences, use the sliders to distribute 100 points across the two sentences and something else. The sliders will automatically snap back if you try to assign more than 100 points.

To continue, please answer the following question:

Can the girl see the contents of the gumball machine?
