InterActive Language Processing Lab at Stanford

In this experiment, you will see several videos and a series of visual scenes and will be asked to answer some questions about them. You will need to listen what the people in the video have to say, so please make sure that your sound is turned on. We recommend Google Chrome or Firefox. The HIT should take ~20 minutes. Please pay attention, thanks!
